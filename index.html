Here's my CV and my contact info.

Research

I study machine learning ecosystems where ML models (such as LLMs) interact with humans, other ML models, and model-providers. I aim to steer how multi-agent interactions shape performance trends and societal outcomes. To tackle this, my work traces ecosystem-level outcomes back to the incentives of interacting agents and to the pipeline for training, evaluating, and deploying ML models. For example, we investigate scaling laws under competition, feedback loops with LLM agents, and the market of model-providers training LLMs.
